    About Prediction Pulse

    📊

    Real Dataset

    Trained on 1,825 real patient records with 13 clinical features including BP readings, symptoms, medication status, and medical history. 80/20 train-test split with stratified sampling.

    🤖

    Why Logistic Regression

    Decision Tree, Random Forest, and SVM all achieved 100% — a classic overfiting indicator. LR's 97.3% with excellent cross-validation stability makes it the clinically safe, generalizable choice.

    🩺

    BP Classification Stages

    • Normal (systolic <120, diastolic <80)
    • Stage 1 (130—139 / 80—89)
    • Stage 2 (≥ 140 / ≥ 90)
    • Crisis (≥ 180 / ≥ 120)
